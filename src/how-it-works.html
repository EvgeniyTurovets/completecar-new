Filing a Claim

How It Works

If you need to file a repair claim for an item covered under your vehicle service contract just follow these 3 steps.

All claims must be called in and approved before any work is performed. You run the risk of the repair not being authorized for payment if you do not get approval prior to the work beginning.

Get Towed

When you experience a breakdown, just contact your Roadside Assistance provider to get your vehicle towed to the ASE-Certified® repair facility of your choice.

GET A FREE QUOTE

Authorize

Once your vehicle has been towed to the ASE-Certified® repair facility of your choice, contact your claims department as provided by your plan administrator.

GET A FREE QUOTE

Payment

Covered claims will be paid directly to the ASE-Certified® repair facility by your contract administrator. You only pay your deductible plus any non-covered charges.
